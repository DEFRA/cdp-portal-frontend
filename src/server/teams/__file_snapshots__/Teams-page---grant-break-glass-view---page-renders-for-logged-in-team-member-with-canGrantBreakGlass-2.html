Break glass production access

Granting temporary break glass access will allow B. A. Baracus to access any services owned by the Mock Team team in production via the CDP Terminal.

This access is valid for 2 hours and will automatically expire. All actions will be logged and audited. Misuse may result in disciplinary action and/or legal consequences. See Break Glass Production Access via Terminal for more details.

Security clearance requirement

Break glass access can only be granted to users with valid UK Government Security Clearance (SC).

The grantor B. A. Baracus must:

  • Hold a valid SC
  • Confirm with Defra's security vetting organisation SecurityVetting@defra.gov.uk that the grantee B. A. Baracus also holds valid SC

Declaration

By granting access, I confirm that:

  • I, the grantor B. A. Baracus, hold a valid SC
  • I have confirmed that the grantee B. A. Baracus holds a valid SC
  • All activity will be logged for audit purposes
  • Misuse may result in disciplinary action and/or legal consequences
  • PII data in production will be handled appropriately, especially if copied to a local machine
  • The work is essential and cannot reasonably be delayed or carried out in another way
